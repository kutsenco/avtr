premier

A new one was presented at the CES 2020 exhibition conceptual Mercedes-Benz – VISION AVTR. Futuristic concept of the presented The electric car unites man and nature and the car in a unique way.

Ola Kallenius, chairman of the board of Daimler AG and Mercedes-Benz AG, in their statement presented the concept car together with the laureate Oscar winner and creator of the film Avatar, James Cameron.

It is the philosophy created by the iconic director in a fantastic film, became the basis of creation VISION AVTR.

"Mercedes-Benz has always been one of the best technological premium brands. Now the time has come to combine luxury and environmental friendliness. After all, that's the only way we can match standards of the future".

INTERIOR & EXTERIOR

The VISION AVTR design is the embodiment of environmental friendliness

The innovative concept of VISION AVTR combines ecological interaction of interior and exterior design and UX. The entire design process was focused on the concrete result is a unique experience of interaction and perception of the concept car by the driver and passengers. Thing was about creating a unique space in which passengers have a biometric connection with each other, with transport means and the surrounding world.

The lateral outer opening passes through the inner part and creates an endless loop, the prototype of which was the sacred one the connection between the Na'va people in the movie Avatar and their natural environment

Also, thanks to the elongated "One Bow" design and organic philosophy of design, VISION AVTR demonstrates a radically new stage of automobile construction. Thus, passing the path from the concept of awareness to unique fusion of a person and a car, to engineers Mercedes-Benz managed to create a concept electric car VISION AVTR.

Laconic continuation of the external minimalist The front seats, made in in a very organic form, which resembles foliage hammocks from the planet Pandora. The central console symbolizes The Tree of Souls, the Na'va's most sacred place. Control unit - intuitive and incredibly functional.

In addition, the control center is located so that it is easily accessible both for the driver and for the passenger, and also for passengers in the back.

Thus, VISION AVTR establishes close biometric connection with the driver and helps increase awareness of the environment situations

The back of the seats and the interior of the roof, made of a special fabric that changes color. Depending on the lighting, the textile can be dark blue or delicate light blue color.

But in addition, the eco-friendliness of the interior of the electric car is improved with the help of DINAMICA® vegetarian leather - the first and only one microfiber, which guarantees environmental efficiency on throughout the entire production cycle.

DINAMICA® is a recycled material from old clothes, fabrics and PET plastic bottles.
